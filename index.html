About us

Gupteshwor Multiple Campus was established in 1991 A.D. with joint effort of the people of Shivalaya VDC and other VDCs in the neighborhood. It is in the headquarter of Parbat district, Kushma, about 270 km. far from the capital city Kathmandu and about 60 km far from Pokhara, from the establishment of this campus, public donation has been the major source of fund. This campus is affiliated with Tribhuvan University and Higher Secondary Education Board of Nepal. About 27 ropanies of land sponsored by Shivalaya VDC is occupied by this campus. The classes and administration of this campus are now running in four buildings which were built on the year 2002 A.D, 2003 A.D and 2007 A.D. The fund for the construction of the two old buildings was collected as the donation of the public charity and new two buildings were constructed as the financial assistance of Indian Embassy. Altogether this campus has 36 rooms where all the classes are running. Moreover, the library, administration, account and exam procedures have been managed in these rooms. In the beginning of its establishment only the classes of Intermediate level affiliated to Tribhuvan University were running in the morning shift in the buildings of local school namely Narayan Hihger Secondary School.
